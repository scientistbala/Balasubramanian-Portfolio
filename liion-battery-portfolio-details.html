Li-ion Battery Customization

Description

Designing and optimizing lightweight, high-capacity Li-ion battery packs for UAV applications. With a focus on achieving maximum endurance, minimum weight, and superior performance, the designs include a variety of configurations suitable for diverse UAV platforms.

Key Achivements:

  • 20000mAh 6S Battery Pack: Achieved exceptional endurance for larger UAV platforms while maintaining efficient weight-to-power ratios.
  • 10000mAh 4S Battery Pack: Designed for compact drones, offering optimal performance with a weight of only 600g.
  • Custom Casing Designs: Engineered protective and ventilated casings to ensure battery safety, heat dissipation, and durability during UAV operations.
  • Lightweight Material Use: Successfully reduced battery pack weight by integrating 3D-printed casing designs with advanced material selection.
Technical Details:

UAV endurance enhancement, lightweight power systems for VTOL drones, and hybrid systems.

Techniques Used:

  • Advanced CAD for casing and weight optimization.
  • Spot welding and soldering for seamless battery cell integration.
  • Heat management and safety enhancements.
Client Benefits:

  • Increased Flight Time: Enhanced UAV operation times by up to 20%.
  • Durability: Improved structural integrity of battery casings to withstand operational wear and tear.
  • Cost Efficiency: Provided reliable and high-performance battery solutions at optimized costs.
