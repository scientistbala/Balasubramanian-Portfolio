Advanced Manufacturing Techniques

Description

Leveraging expertise in FDM, SLS, and MJF printing technologies, I have designed and fabricated precision parts and optimized lightweight components for UAVs and industrial applications. From concept to prototype, my work consistently meets high-performance standards.

Key Achivements:

  • Lightweight Design: Produced UAV gimbal housings and camera mounts with weights as low as 10.5g while ensuring durability.
  • Material Excellence: Delivered high-performance parts using PA12 and carbon-reinforced polymers for superior strength.
  • Custom UAV Fabrication: Engineered drone arms, frames, and payload carriers, reducing weight by 30% while maintaining structural integrity.
  • Rapid Prototyping: Achieved fully functional prototypes with a fast turnaround, adapting designs seamlessly to client requirements.
Impact:

My 3D printing expertise bridges design and manufacturing, enabling innovation in UAV development and industrial-grade solutions, pushing boundaries in strength, weight, and performance.
