Guardian- The AI Quad

Description

The Guardian, a fully autonomous quadcopter, is revolutionizing aerial surveillance and inspection with advanced AI features and 4G/5G connectivity. Designed entirely from scratch, the Guardian is built for efficiency, accuracy, and ease of use.

Key Features:

  • High-Resolution Surveillance: Equipped with a 4K 100x zoom camera (10x optical, 10x digital).
  • AI Video Analytics: Real-time analysis and automatic alerts using cloud-based AI via 4G/5G.
  • Long Endurance: Up to 60 minutes flight time with a thermal camera payload.
  • Payload Flexibility: Supports up to 400g payloads, including thermal and zoom cameras.
  • Secure Streaming: Streams live video securely via 4G/5G with onboard 128GB storage.
Applications:

  • Security & Surveillance
  • Disaster Response Support
  • Perimeter and Fence Line Monitoring
  • Search and Rescue Operations
  • Solar Panel Inspections
