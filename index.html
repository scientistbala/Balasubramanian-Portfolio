Services

UAV Design & Development

From concept to creation, I specialize in crafting high-performance UAVs tailored to meet specific industry needs.

  • End-to-end mechanical design using tools like SolidWorks and Fusion 360.
  • Aerodynamic optimization for superior endurance and efficiency.
  • Custom drone prototyping and manufacturing.

Battery Pack Design & Optimization

I design lightweight, high-capacity Li-ion battery packs for UAVs, ensuring optimal performance and longer flight times.

  • Customized battery configurations to suit specific payloads and flight durations.
  • Weight optimization and thermal management solutions.

UAV Prototyping & Manufacturing

With hands-on expertise in 3D printing, composite manufacturing, and assembly, I bring UAV designs to life with precision and reliability.

  • Prototype development for testing and iteration.
  • Scalable manufacturing for deployment-ready systems.

Sensor Integration & Payload Solutions

I integrate advanced sensors and payload systems for applications like LiDAR surveys, detect-and-avoid functionalities, and real-time video streaming.

  • Precision alignment and calibration for payload efficiency.
  • Seamless hardware-software integration for enhanced data accuracy.

Drone Data Processing

Transform raw UAV data into actionable insights using industry-leading tools like Pix4D and QT Modeler.

  • LiDAR data preprocessing and analysis.
  • Geotagging and 3D modeling solutions.

Training & Consultation

Empowering businesses and individuals with the skills and knowledge to excel in UAV operations.

  • Virtual and hands-on drone workshops.
  • Expert consultation for UAV project planning, design, and execution.
